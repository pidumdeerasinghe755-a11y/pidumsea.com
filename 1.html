The Wonders of the Sea

Discover the beauty, mystery, and depth of our planet's vast oceans.

Our Blue Planet

Oceans cover more than 70% of the Earth's surface. They regulate our climate, provide a home to millions of species, and hold secrets that humans are still exploring today.

The Sunlight Zone

The top layer of the ocean where light penetrates. It's bustling with life, from colorful coral reefs to sea turtles and playful dolphins.

The Twilight Zone

As you go deeper, light begins to fade. Creatures here have adapted to low-light conditions, and many can glow in the dark!

The Midnight Zone

Complete and total darkness. The water is freezing, and the pressure is immense, yet bizarre and resilient creatures thrive here.

Amazing Ocean Facts

  • The Pacific Ocean is wider than the Moon.
  • We have explored less than 10% of the global ocean.
  • The Mariana Trench is the deepest known point, plunging nearly 11,000 meters down.
  • The world's longest mountain chain is actually underwater, known as the Mid-Ocean Ridge.
